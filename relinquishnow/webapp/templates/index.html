About RelinquishNow

RelinquishNow is a non profit organization aiming providing support for the less privileged. Our vision is to provide a platform where people can get resources from a repository of donated items and donators have transparency of items donated. Our aim is to eliminate food and resource wastage and distribute it to those who need it.
